tom and jerry properties

  • 161 theatrical short films: These short films were produced by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer from 1940 to 1967. They won seven Academy Awards, making them the most-awarded animated series in history.
  • Television shows: There have been several television shows based on Tom and Jerry, including The Tom and Jerry Show (1965-1972), The Tom and Jerry Comedy Show (1980-1982), and Tom and Jerry Tales (2006-2008).
  • Direct-to-video films: There have been over 30 direct-to-video films based on Tom and Jerry, including Tom and Jerry: The Movie (1992), Tom and Jerry: The Magic Ring (2002), and Tom and Jerry: Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory (2017).
  • Video games: There have been over 50 video games based on Tom and Jerry, including Tom and Jerry: The Movie (1993), Tom and Jerry: Chase (2001), and Tom and Jerry: War of the Whiskers (2002).
  • Merchandise: There is a wide variety of Tom and Jerry merchandise available, including toys, clothing, home goods, and more.
